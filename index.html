Our Mission:

What is FIRST?

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a program for students of all ages to gain experience in science, technology, and robotics which helps to prepare them for the future. FIRST offers 3 flagship programs which all challenge students to develop robots specifically designed to complete a task provided at the beginning of the season.

Competitions and Seasonal events

While the club lasts all year long we don't actualy start building our robot until January when we are given the rules and objectives for the competition. Then the 8 week build period starts and we build our robot to the critera of the challenge given by FIRST robotics.

Outreach

Our robotics team goes to community events or hosts events where we introduce future robotics members to our robot and the idea of working on team. We are eager for you to reach out and invite us to speak at your event, click here to book us for your event.
